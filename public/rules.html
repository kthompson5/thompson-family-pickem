Kickoff Edition Rules

  • Pick the winner of each college football and NFL Week 1 matchup.
  • 1 point awarded for each correct pick.
  • Picks lock before the first kickoff of each league (College & NFL).
  • Maximum of 2 submissions per player (use them wisely!).
  • $5 entry per submission. Payouts will be split among top finishers and will be announced once entries are final.
  • Track your standings live on the Leaderboard page.
  • Check out the Game Info page for pick trends and analytics during the weekend!

Tiebreakers

  • 1st: Total points in the Sunday Night NFL game.
  • 2nd: Fewest incorrect NFL picks.
  • 3rd: Fewest incorrect College picks.
  • 4th: Random coin flip conducted by Em & Elliot.
